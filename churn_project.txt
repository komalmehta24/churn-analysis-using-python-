# Case Study: ConnectTel Customer Churn Analysis

##  Executive Summary

**Company**: ConnectTel Communications  
**Analysis Period**: 2024  
**Analyst**: Data Analytics Team  
**Problem**: 26.5% annual customer churn rate costing $4M+ annually  

**Key Finding**: Month-to-month contracts drive 4.5x higher churn than annual contracts

**Top Recommendation**: Convert high-risk month-to-month customers to annual contracts, saving $1.1M in first year



## 1. Business Problem

### The Situation
ConnectTel is losing customers faster than industry average. With 7,043 total customers, we lost 1,869 customers last year.

### The Impact
- **Revenue Loss**: $2.8M in annual recurring revenue
- **Acquisition Cost**: $1.2M to replace lost customers
- **Total Annual Loss**: $4.0M

### Why This Matters
- Every 1% reduction in churn saves $150,000
- Customer lifetime value is 3x higher for loyal customers
- Competitors are gaining market share
